--- title: The Shortlist ---

Looking for a short list of stuff to read? Here's some of the stuff I'm most proud of.

Fiction

  • Disappearance

    A weasel attempts to escape from her life in Sawtooth to Oregon, but finds her old life still tied to home. Part of the Sawtooth universe.

  • Overclassification

    A chronically neat otter's life is thrown into disarray when she takes a homeless ringtail in from the cold. Part of the Sawtooth universe.

Interactive Fiction

  • You're Gone

    A husband grieves the loss of his wife through instant messages. Part of the Sawtooth universe.

  • Gallery Exhibition: A Love Story

    A twine game taking place within the Post-Self universe. Dear, Also, The Tree That Was Felled holds a gallery exhibition of its works as an instance artist. Part of the Post-Self universe.

Poetry

  • Growth

    A lengthy poem about the end of an era.

  • Beneath her coat was a whole identity

    First place winner in the Typewriter Anthologies poetry contest.

Non-fiction

  • New American Absinthe

    An article about the up-and-coming absinthe culture in the US.

  • Gender: Furry - An investigation into the interplay of gender and fandom

    An essay about the complex interactions of gender and identity in the furry subculture.
