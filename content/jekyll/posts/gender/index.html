--- layout: category title: On Gender ---

Gender is a big thing. A huge thing. It's bigger than a lot of people give it credit, and I think that's something that I started to understand in my early twenties, when it started to cause some distress for me. It's not a binary, and even calling it a spectrum seems to deny a lot of the depth behind the social and psychological construction that is gender.

I've been exploring gender for a while now in a few different ways, but am only just starting to write about it. I started with a Tumblr blog, which I've moved here (though it's still mirrored there), but will be adding additional writings as time goes on.
