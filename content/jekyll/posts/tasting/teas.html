Welcome to Makyo's Master List of Teas! This is the list of teas that I have in my stash broken down in various different ways. As I drink the teas or buy new ones, they'll be updated on the spreadsheet below. When a tea runs out, it'll remain on the spread sheet but its "Amount left" column will be 0. As I buy more of a tea, I will update both the original amount and amount left (this won't be a purchase log, in other words). I'm trying to stay on top of tasting notes, but I know I've been bad about it. I'll be better!
