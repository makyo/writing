One of the best ways to internalize something important, I've always felt, is to write about it. If ever there's something that weighs on me, something that means a lot to me for no clear reason, or something that I just don't quite understand, I've always had good luck with writing about it.

This site is for that. It allows me to explore through writing all of those things that interest me as mentioned. The format is longer than a blog, allowing what I write to stand on its own. If ever, for some reason, you wish to contact me about any of this, feel free to send me an email at makyo@drab-makyo.com.
